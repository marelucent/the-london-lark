About

Not all maps name the magic.

Not all doors bear a sign.

But the city has corners that lean in close

when they know you are the kind...

I know the places that change you. Not the ones on the lists. The other ones — the rooms that hum with something, the corners where strangers become friends, the doors that only open if you know to knock.

I speak in moods, not ratings. Tell me you want somewhere witchy and wild or a place to feel alive or I don't know, surprise me — and I'll show you three cards from the deck.

I carry 23 arcana — ways of being in the city. The Fool for playful and weird. The Hermit for stillness. The Devil for late nights that linger. The Empress for folk and warmth.

And the 23rd? That's mine. The Lark — for romanticised London herself, the city as muse.

Each one holds venues that match its spirit.

I notice when you're carrying something. I'm not a therapist, but I know which doors might help — and when to point you toward humans who can hold what I can't.

I'm for the seekers. The tender rebels. The mystically-minded and the melancholic. Anyone tired of scrolling endless reviews. People looking for places with soul.
