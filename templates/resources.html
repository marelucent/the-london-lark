If You're in Crisis Right Now

If you're thinking of hurting yourself, or you're in immediate danger:

Emergency Services 999 For immediate, life-threatening emergencies
Samaritans 116 123 (free, 24/7) Whatever you're going through, they'll listen without judgment Email: jo@samaritans.org
Crisis Text Line Text SHOUT to 85258 (free, 24/7) Text-based support when speaking feels too hard
NHS 111 111 (press option 2) 24/7 mental health support and urgent care guidance
